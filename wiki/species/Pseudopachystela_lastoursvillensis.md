# *Pseudopachystela lastoursvillensis* Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Cauliflorous shrub with huge stipulate leaves, dense glomerules of white
subsessed flowers on old wood, and a swollen 5-pored stigma. Recorded from
Macoucou, Ighaca, and Moupata.
