# *Diospyros guineensis* (Schumach. & Thonn.) E.Mestre, Meeprom, H.N.Rakouth & Lowry

**Family**: [[Ebenaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Diospyros]]

Raised in the 2025 revision from the old African *D. ferrea* complex. This is
the coastal species north of the equator from Ghana to Benin.

## Diagnosis

A small shrub with symmetric elliptic to slightly obovate leaves,
**1.3-8.6 x 1-5.5 cm**, often with a pair of basal glands, and ellipsoid to
slightly ovoid fruits **8-12 x 6-8 mm**. In the article key it represents the
coastal, northern-equatorial counterpart to the southern coastal species
[[Diospyros_angolensis]] and [[Diospyros_suaheliensis]].

## Distribution

**Ghana, Ivory Coast, and Benin**.

## Habitat and ecology

**Coastal sites** on sandy or rocky soils behind dunes, close to sea level.

## Conservation

Assessed in the protologue as **Vulnerable (VU B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv,v))**.
