# *Synsepalum longecuneatum* De Wild.

Widespread Gabonese shrub or small tree with glabrous long-cuneate leaves and
short axillary fascicles. Recorded from Ngwasso, Imeno, Haut-Ntem, Equata,
Oguemoue, and the lake region.
