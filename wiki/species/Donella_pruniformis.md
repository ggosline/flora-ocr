# *Donella pruniformis* (Pierre ex Engl.) Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Polymorphic large forest tree with variable leaf and fruit shape; the treatment
treats numerous named forms as one species. Recorded from Libreville, Batanga,
Poungou, and Sindara.
