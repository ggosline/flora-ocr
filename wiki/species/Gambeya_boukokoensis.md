# *Gambeya boukokoensis* Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Inland forest tree with grey-white tomentose lower leaf surface and small
globose fruits about 2 cm across. The treatment separates it from
[[Gambeya_africana]] mainly by the grey rather than ferruginous indument and
smaller fruits. Recorded from Lastoursville and Bitam.
