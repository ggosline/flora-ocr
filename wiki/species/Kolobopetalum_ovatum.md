# *Kolobopetalum ovatum* Stapf

**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Kolobopetalum]]

J. Linn. Soc., Bot. **37**: 82 (1905).

## Description

Liana up to **10 m** tall; stem up to 4 cm in diameter. **Leaves**: petiole
3–9 cm; blade ovate-elliptic, **5–10(–15) × 3–6(–7.5) cm**, **cuneate to
obtuse at base** (not cordate), acuminate over 0.6–1.5 cm at apex, with 4 or
5(6) pairs of lateral nerves.

**Male inflorescence** up to 20 cm long, with flowers fascicled by (2)3 or
4(5); bracts narrowly triangular, 1–2 mm. **Female inflorescence** up to 15
cm long, with **solitary flowers**. **Male flower**: pedicel 1–3 mm; outer
sepals ovate, 1 × 0.6–0.7 mm; **inner sepals free**, broadly ovate,
(1.5–)2–2.5(–3) × 1.2–2 mm; petals elliptic, 1.2–1.5 × 0.5 mm, with involute
margin; **stamens 6**, as long as the petals, filaments slightly fused at
base. **Female flower**: pedicel 1 mm; outer and inner sepals as in male;
petals slightly shorter than the carpels; carpels narrowly ellipsoid, 1 mm
long.

**Fruit**: mericarp ovoid, **15 × 11 mm**; endocarp with **pointed spines**.

## Distribution

**Range**: Guinea, Sierra Leone, Liberia, Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, Nigeria,
Gabon, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Central
African Republic.
**Gabon**: Estuaire, Haut-Ogooué, Ogooué-Ivindo.

## Habitat and ecology

Secondary forest and gallery forest. In Gabon up to 530 m altitude, elsewhere
up to 750 m.

## Notes

- Distinguished from [[Kolobopetalum_auriculatum]] by the **non-cordate**
  leaf base (cuneate to obtuse) and from [[Kolobopetalum_synsepalum]] by
  the **free** inner male sepals (vs. fused into a calyx) and the larger
  fruit (15 × 11 mm vs. 7–10 mm).
