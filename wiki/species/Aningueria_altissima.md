# *Aningueria altissima* (A. Chev.) Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Large buttressed tree up to about 40 m with translucent-dotted leaves, fascicled
flowers, and ellipsoid one-seeded fruits. The treatment records Gabon material
from Libreville and Tchibanga.
