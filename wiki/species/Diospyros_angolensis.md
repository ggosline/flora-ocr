# *Diospyros angolensis* E.Mestre, Meeprom, H.N.Rakouth & Lowry, *sp. nov.*

**Family**: [[Ebenaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Diospyros]]

One of five African species segregated in the 2025 revision of material
formerly placed in [[Diospyros_ferrea]]. It is the only member of that complex
recognized from Angola.

## Type

**Holotype**: Angola, Luanda Province, coastal region, 1903, fruiting,
*Gossweiler 789* (P); isotypes BM, FHO, K.

## Diagnosis

Recognized by its small, symmetric leaves with dark pitted glands in the
proximal half of the blade, usually obtuse to rounded apices, and nearly
globose fruits about **7-8 x 7-8(-9) mm** with a small 3-lobed fruiting calyx.

## Distribution

**Known only from Angola**.

## Habitat and ecology

Collected in **coastal and riverine forest**, apparently at low elevation.

## Conservation

Assessed in the protologue as **Endangered (EN B2ab(i,ii,iii,iv,v))**.
