# *Kolobopetalum* spec. nov.

**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Kolobopetalum]]

A possibly undescribed species, recorded once in Gabon. The single sterile
collection makes confident generic placement difficult.

## Description

**Twining liana** reaching at least 7 m; branches and leaves **glabrous**.
**Leaves**: petiole subcylindric, wrinkled when dry, **twisted and/or
curved-geniculate at base**, 6–7 cm; blade broadly ovate, **8–9 × 6–7 cm**,
coriaceous, **truncate and clearly peltate at base**, caudate-acuminate over
1 cm at apex, with **4 pairs of lateral nerves of which a dominant basal
pair**, **with black spots in the axils beneath**.

Male and female inflorescences, flower and fruit unknown.

## Distribution

**Endemic to Gabon**, collected once in Moyen-Ogooué.

## Habitat and ecology

Forest gap on a slope in degraded forest, at 45 m altitude.

## Notes

- The single sterile collection leaves the **identification uncertain**:
  it could be a new species in [[Kolobopetalum]] or in *Rhigiocarya* (see
  [[Rhigiocarya_racemifera]]). The leaf texture favours *Kolobopetalum*,
  but more material is needed.
- The **clearly peltate** leaf base (vs. obscurely peltate at most in
  *K. auriculatum*) leads to this entity in the [[Kolobopetalum]] key.
