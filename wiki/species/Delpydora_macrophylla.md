# *Delpydora macrophylla* Pierre

Hairy subshrub with very large auriculate-based leaves, rusty hirsute flowers,
and subglobose hirsute fruits. The treatment records it from Libreville, Kango,
Echiras forest, Imeno, and Tchibanga.
