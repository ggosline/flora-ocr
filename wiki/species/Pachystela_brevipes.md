# *Pachystela brevipes* (Bak.) Engl.

Widespread riparian small tree or shrub with persistent filiform stipules,
variable narrow leaves, and yellow ellipsoid one-seeded fruits. Recorded from
Njole, Lambarene, Tchibanga, Lastoursville, and the lake region.
