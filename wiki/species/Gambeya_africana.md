# *Gambeya africana* (Don ex Baker) Pierre

Variable large-fruited tree complex with many ferruginous-tomentose nerves and
edible yellow-orange fruits. The treatment keeps the Gabon material under this
species and records it from Djole, Kango, Tchibanga, and Sindara.
