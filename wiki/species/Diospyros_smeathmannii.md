# *Diospyros smeathmannii* (A.DC.) E.Mestre, Meeprom, H.N.Rakouth & Lowry

**Family**: [[Ebenaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Diospyros]]

The widespread west-to-central African segregate recognized in the 2025
revision of the old African [[Diospyros_ferrea]] complex.

## Diagnosis

Separated by often asymmetric or somewhat falcate leaves with **scattered
minute glands** rather than a clear basal pair, and ellipsoid fruits
**10-14 x 5-9 mm**. The article treats it as the only widespread member of the
complex and the one that overlaps geographically with [[Diospyros_moutsambotei]].

## Distribution

From **Senegal to northeastern DRC**. The article also documents
**Gabonese** records from the **Estuaire** area around Ovang east of
Libreville.

## Habitat and ecology

Most often on **river and stream banks** and in **swamps**, usually from sea
level to about **500 m**, rarely to **1250 m**.

## Conservation

Assessed in the protologue as **Least Concern (LC)**.
