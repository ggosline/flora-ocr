# *Anisocycla jollyana* (Pierre) Diels

**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]] · **Genus**: [[Anisocycla]]

in Engl., Pflanzenr. **IV.94**: 94 (1910).

## Synonymy

- *Glossopholis jollyana* Pierre, Bull. Soc. Linn. Paris n.s. **1**: 84 (1898).

## Description

**Erect shrub**, only **30–70 cm tall** — one of only two non-climbing
Menispermaceae in Gabon (the other being [[Penianthus_longifolius|*Penianthus
longifolius*]]). Twigs, petioles and lower midrib **puberulent**, glabrescent
or not. **Leaves**: petiole **0.8–1.6 cm**, thickened distally; blade
obovate-elliptic, **7–16 × (2–)4–6(–8) cm**, rounded at base, acuminate over
5–10(–20) mm at apex, with 7–11 pairs of lateral nerves, midrib prominent on
both faces, glabrous above.

**Male inflorescence** axillary to terminal, up to 3 cm long, puberulent;
peduncle 1.5 cm; bracts and bracteoles ± linear, 0.5–1 mm. **Male flower**:
pedicel 0–0.5 mm; sepals **± 15** in three series — 6 outer ± ovate ± 1 mm,
puberulent without; 3 median 1.5–2 × 1 mm, sparsely puberulent to glabrous;
6 inner oblong-elliptic, 2–2.5 × 1–1.5 mm, glabrous; petals 5 or 6, ovate, ±
0.5 mm, glabrous; **synandrium ± globose**, with stipe up to 1 mm, glabrous,
with ± 15 anthers.

**Female flower** unknown. **Fruit**: mericarp ellipsoidal, **15–22 × 10–19
mm, orange**, glabrous; mesocarp 1–2 mm thick; endocarp obovoid to
ellipsoidal, 10–15 × 9–13 mm, slightly flattened, reticulate; **condyle
lamelliform**.

## Distribution

**Range**: Cameroon, Equatorial Guinea (Rio Muni), Gabon, Democratic Republic
of the Congo.
**Gabon**: Estuaire, Ngounié, Nyanga.

## Habitat and ecology

Understorey of streamside and terra firma forest, up to 650 m altitude.

## Notes

- *Anisocycla* is monospecific in Gabon. Together with
  [[Penianthus_longifolius]] this is one of only **two non-liana**
  Menispermaceae in the Gabonese flora — and the **smaller** of the two,
  being a true shrub of 30–70 cm.
