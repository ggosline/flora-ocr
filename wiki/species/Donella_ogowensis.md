# *Donella ogowensis* (A. Chev.) Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Riparian Ogooue-basin tree with narrow non-acuminate leaves, tiny glabrous
flowers, and yellow elongated 5-seeded fruits. The treatment says it is always
found by water.
