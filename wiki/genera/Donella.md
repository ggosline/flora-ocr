# *Donella* Pierre ex Baill.

Genus of many-fine-nerved glabrous-leaved Sapotaceae kept separate from
[[Gambeya]] by the treatment mainly on leaf venation and anatomy. Four species
are treated from Gabon: [[Donella_ogowensis]], [[Donella_pentagonocarpa]],
[[Donella_pruniformis]], and [[Donella_welwitschii]].
