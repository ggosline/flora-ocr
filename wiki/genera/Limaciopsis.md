# *Limaciopsis*

**Authority**: Engl., Bot. Jahrb. Syst. **26**: 414 (1899).
**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]]

## Diagnosis

**Lianas**, puberulent to tomentellous, glabrescent. Leaves simple,
petiolate; blade entire-margined. Inflorescences in axillary panicles,
**often up to four in series**. Flowers shortly pedicellate; sepals 6, in
2 whorls, hairy; petals 6, shorter than the sepals, hairy outside, with
partly involute margin. Male flowers: **stamens 6, free**, slightly
incurved, glabrous. Female flowers: staminodes absent; carpels 3,
tomentose. Fruits with smooth mericarps; endocarp bony, **horseshoe-shaped**.
Seeds with little albumen.

A **monospecific** genus of central Africa.
