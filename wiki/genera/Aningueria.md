# *Aningueria* Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Large-tree genus with translucent-dotted leaves, urceolate corollas, extrorse
apiculate anthers, and one-seeded fruits with a very broad ventral scar. One
species is treated from Gabon: [[Aningueria_altissima]].
