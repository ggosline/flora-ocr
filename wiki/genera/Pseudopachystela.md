# *Pseudopachystela* Aubrev. & Pellegr.

Thick-sepaled genus with long staminodes, a stout style, and a swollen
5-pored stigma. The treatment recognizes 2 Gabonese species:
[[Pseudopachystela_lastoursvillensis]] and [[Pseudopachystela_oyemensis]].
