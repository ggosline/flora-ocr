# *Wildemaniodoxa* Aubrev. & Pellegr.

**Authority**: Aubrev. & Pellegr.  
**Family**: [[Sapotaceae]]

## Diagnosis

The treatment describes this as the only African Sapotaceae with flowers having
**10 petals**, **10 stamens** inserted at the level of corolla-lobe fusion, and
an ovary with **10 uniovulate locules**. Staminodes are rudimentary and
irregular in number. Leaves are tomentose with many lateral veins. Fruit was
unknown.

construction that the author treats as fully diagnostic.

## Notes

- The source explicitly says the fruit remains unknown.
- Leaf similarity with [[Zeyherella_longepedicellata]] is noted, but the flower
  structure is completely different.
