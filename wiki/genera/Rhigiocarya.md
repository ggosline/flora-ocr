# *Rhigiocarya*

**Authority**: Miers, Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist., ser. 3, **14**: 100 (1864).
**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]]

## Diagnosis

**Completely glabrous lianas**. Leaves simple; petiole twisted/curved or
geniculate at base; blade entire-margined. Inflorescences: **male in
racemiform panicles**, **female in racemes**. Male flowers: sepals 6,
free; petals 6; **stamens 6**, the 3 outer with free filaments or fused at
the base, the 3 inner with **± completely fused filaments**, thecae
dehiscing longitudinally. Female flowers: sepals and petals ± like the
male; carpels 3. Fruits: mericarps with bony endocarp bearing **subtruncate
apical spines**. Seeds with ruminate albumen.

A tropical African genus of **4 species**, of which 3 in Upper Guinea, one
(this) in Lower Guinea and Congolia.
