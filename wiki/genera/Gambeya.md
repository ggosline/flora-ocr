# *Gambeya* Pierre

African multiseeded fruit genus with non-stipulate leaves, spaced secondary
veins, and short-cylindric corollas. The treatment treats 3 species as
confirmed in Gabon: [[Gambeya_boukokoensis]], [[Gambeya_subnuda]], and
[[Gambeya_africana]].
