# *Sarcolophium*

**Authority**: Troupin, Bull. Jard. Bot. État Bruxelles **30**: 30 (1960).
**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]]

## Diagnosis

**Lianas with subereous (corky) branches**. Leaves simple; blade with
**3 to 5 basal nerves**. Inflorescences in panicles with **solitary
flowers**. Male flowers: sepals 6, the inner larger than the outer; petals
6, ± of equal length, the inner with two crests on the inner face;
**stamens 6, with free, arched, dilated filaments**, thecae dehiscing
longitudinally. **Female flowers unknown**. Fruits with smooth mericarps;
**endocarp covered with intertwined crests**. Seeds albuminous.

A **monospecific** genus of central Africa.
