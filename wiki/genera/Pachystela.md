# *Pachystela* Pierre ex Engl.

Small trees or shrubs with corolla lobes longer than the tube, stamens as long
as the lobes, no staminodes, and one-seeded fruits. The treatment recognizes 2
Gabonese species: [[Pachystela_brevipes]] and [[Pachystela_buluensis]].
