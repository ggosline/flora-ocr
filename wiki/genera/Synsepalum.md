# *Synsepalum* (A. DC.) Baill.

Partially fused calyx, petaloid well-developed staminodes, and one-seeded
fruits with a broad ventral scar define this genus in the treatment. It treats
4 Gabonese species as present: [[Synsepalum_le-testui]],
[[Synsepalum_longecuneatum]], [[Synsepalum_congolense]], and
[[Synsepalum_fleuryanum]].
