# *Dialytheca*

**Authority**: Exell & Mendonça, J. Bot. **73**, Suppl. Polypet. **1**: 8
(1935).
**Family**: [[Menispermaceae]]

## Diagnosis

**Glabrous lianas**. Leaves simple, petiolate; blade entire-margined.
Inflorescences: **male in racemes with cymules of (1)2 or 3 flowers**;
**female unknown**. Male flowers: sepals 6, free, imbricate; petals 6,
free; **stamens 3**, with fused filaments and an enlarged, **triangular
connective**, anthers extrorse. **Female flowers and fruits unknown.**

A **monospecific** genus of central Africa.
