# Flore du Gabon — Volume 60 (2022)

**Editors**: Marc S.M. Sosef, Jacques Florence, Henri Paul Bourobou Bourobou,
Pulchérie Bissiengou.
**Publisher**: Margraf Publishers, Weikersheim · Backhuys Publishers, Leiden.
**ISBN**: 978-3-8236-1807-2 · **ISSN**: 0071-5883.
**DOI**: [10.5281/zenodo.14900487](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14900487)

Volume 60 is part of the modern Margraf-published continuation of the
**Flore du Gabon** series originally edited at the Muséum national d'Histoire
naturelle, Paris (1961–2007). The modern series is co-organised by Naturalis
(Leiden), the Meise Botanic Garden, and the Herbier National du Gabon
(IPHAMETRA — CENAREST, Libreville). Volume 60 received additional financial
support from the Alberta Mennega Foundation.
