# Boraginaceae

**Authority**: Juss. (1789), nom. cons.  
**Order**: Boraginales (APG-style broad family concept)

## Diagnosis

Herbs, shrubs, or trees with simple leaves, usually scorpioid cymes, gamopetalous
flowers, and drupaceous or schizocarpic fruits. In Gabon the family is mostly
woody rather than herbaceous, which is unusual relative to the global centre of
diversity.

## Distribution

Boraginaceae in the broad sense contains about 195 genera and 2,750 species
worldwide. In Gabon it is a small family, represented by five genera and nine
species.
